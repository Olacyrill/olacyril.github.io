Why choose OSCCA?

With its strong foundation in creating ONDO STATE CO-OPERATIVE COLLEGE as a great academic centre in the previous century, the State Government established OSCCA to offer a new reality for the young State, in order to harness the possibilities hidden in Cooperatives as has been witnessed throughout the Western World.

The need for Cooperatives has never been more apparent in Nigeria in the light of the current economic realities, therefore, ONDO STATE CO-OPERATIVE COLLEGE is forging ahead with its commitment to provide professional work force for the Cooperative Sector in order to compliment the enabling environment already facilitated by the Department of Cooperative Services. This joint effort is laying a strong foundation for an ecosystem that nurtures young minds to be innovators, entrepreneurs and leaders in the Cooperative space. Thus, building a network of modern cooperators savvy in multiple domains, adopting technology, management and networking skills, proficient in business and the ability to appreciate hard work and integrity, for a sustainable development through Cooperatives in Ondo State.



OUR VISION: To be a reference point of excellence in Cooperative Education, Research and Training, as well as in Innovation & Entrepreneurship and to produce graduates with exceptional leadership qualities to serve state, national and global needs.

OUR MISSION: To achieve our objectives in an environment that enhances creativity, innovation and scholarly pursuits while adhering to our vision and cooperative values.
